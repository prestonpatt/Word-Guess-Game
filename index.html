How to Play

BEFORE YOU TYPE A LETTER!!!

The object of the game is to guess the word represented by the underscores to the right in a hangman style game.

You get 1 guess per character in the unknown Harry Potter related word +3

Using your keyboard type the letters you wish to use as a guess. The first letter you type will start the game.

May the odds be ever in your favor.
